# Hairstyle Library v2

Sourcing approach: This v2 normalizes durable barber, salon, and curl-specialist vocabulary into deterministic app tags, using conservative face-shape fit, texture-specific cutting assumptions, and facial-hair compatibility as recommendation signals rather than universal permissions.

### Length: Buzz

## Classic Buzz Cut

**ID:** `classic-buzz-cut`
**Description:** Even clipper length across the head with a clean, minimal silhouette. It reads direct, tidy, and deliberately low-maintenance.

**Description:** Long hair with broad, soft waves rather than beachy texture or tight curls. The look is gentle, polished, and movement-focused.
**Presentation:** feminine
**Face shapes:** Oval, Round, Square, Oblong, Heart, Diamond
**Hair length required:** Shoulder, Long
**Hair textures:** Straight, Wavy
**Facial hair compatible:** None
**Maintenance:** medium
**Tone:** neutral
**Disallowed if:** Coily hair or tight curls unless heat-styled
**Notes:** Use larger bends for a professional finish and less tousle than beach waves.

## Blowout Layers

**ID:** `blowout-layers`
**Description:** Long or shoulder-length layers styled with smooth volume, lifted roots, and curved ends. It reads polished and high-effort.
**Presentation:** feminine
**Face shapes:** Round, Oblong, Heart, Triangle
**Hair length required:** Shoulder, Long
**Hair textures:** Straight, Wavy
**Facial hair compatible:** None
**Maintenance:** high
**Tone:** professional
**Disallowed if:** User avoids heat styling or has very curly/coily hair
**Notes:** This is a styling-heavy recommendation; cut alone will not create the finish.
